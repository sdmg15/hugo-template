I have finally integrated my Picasa photos that are now available on the Photos page. Blogger doesn't seem to allow you to host anything other than HTML, so I had to host my picasa-app scripts that do the integration externally. To this end I enabled cross-site deployment in picasa-app which was a matter of changing the linker from std to xs. When viewing the photos, there are some glitches related to z-order which I am going to look at later.
